ROIS will elevate your business, setting a new benchmark for boutique offices in Cremorne. The Crowning Glory

Le Sommet has taken the game to a new level. With panoramic views of the CBD this is the perfect place to entertain your clients. Complete with a full bar adorned with marble finishes this will be the place to be in Cremorne. Ensuring that the rooftop is fully weatherproof was at the architects front of mind, with the inclusion of an automatic louvre rooftop that can be adjusted as required.

Workspaces crafted to your needs

The workspace entails perfectly positioned columns to ensure floor space is maximised for work and break-out areas. Clean services have been considered to provide beautiful creative spaces to enhance free thinking and contribute to collaborative work. The exterior has been designed with floor-to-ceiling glass to bring outstanding natural light to each level.
